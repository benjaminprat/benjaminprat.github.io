The Best Band You've Probably Never Heard Of

Spoon is an American indie rock band from Austin, Texas, formed in 1993. After more than 25 years recording, the group has released 9 original albums. The albums, listed in chronological order, are as follows:

  • Telephono
  • A Series of Sneaks
  • Girls Can Tell
  • Kill The Moonlight
  • Kill The Moonlight
  • Gimme Fiction
  • Ga Ga Ga Ga
  • Transference
  • They Want My Soul
  • Hot Thoughts
