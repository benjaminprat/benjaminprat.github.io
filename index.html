The Best Band You've Probably Never Heard Of

Spoon is an American indie rock band from Austin, Texas, formed in 1993. After more than 25 years, the group has released 9 original albums. The albums, in chronological order, are:

  • Telephono
  • A Series of Sneaks
  • Girls Can Tell
  • Kill The Moonlight
  • Gimme Fiction
  • Ga Ga Ga Ga
  • Transference
  • They Want My Soul
  • Hot Thoughts
